What Exactly is SSB?

Services Selection Board (SSB) is a 5-day personality and intelligence assessment process conducted to select future officers for the Indian Armed Forces.

🧠

Psychology Tests

TAT, WAT, SRT and Self Description tests are conducted to evaluate your mindset and Officer Like Qualities.

🎯

Screening Process

PPDT and OIR tests decide whether you proceed for the full 5-day selection process.

💪

GTO Tasks

Group tasks test leadership, communication and teamwork skills.

The 5-Day SSB Process

Day 1

OIR + PPDT Screening

Day 2

TAT, WAT, SRT & SD

Day 3

Group Discussions & GTO Tasks

Day 4

Lecturette & Personal Interview

Day 5

Conference Round
