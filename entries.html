Defence Entries & Exams 🇮🇳

Explore all major entries to join the Indian Armed Forces.

🪖

NDA

National Defence Academy entry after 12th for Army, Navy & Air Force.

  • Age: 16.5 - 19.5
  • Qualification: 12th Pass
  • Exam by UPSC
✈️

AFCAT

Join Indian Air Force through Flying & Technical branches.

  • Graduate Entry
  • Air Force Selection Board
  • Flying Branch
⚓

CDS

Combined Defence Services examination conducted by UPSC.

  • Graduate Entry
  • IMA / OTA / INA
  • SSB Interview
🎖️

NCC Special Entry

Direct SSB entry for NCC cadets with C certificate.

  • No Written Exam
  • Direct SSB
  • Army Entry
